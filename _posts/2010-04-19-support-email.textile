GolfStats now has an email address: "support&#64;golf-stats&#46;net":mailto:support@golf-stats.net.  Please use this email for any support or feature requests you have.

Currently, email is the only way to do the following:
* Add another OpenID provider to your account
* Delete an incorrectly entered round
* Edit your name

You can also use this email address to report any bugs you find, if you prefer not to use the "Launchpad bug tracker":https://bugs.launchpad.net/golfstats.
